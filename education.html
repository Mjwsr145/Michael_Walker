Michael Walker, Sr.

  • 1986 Graduate of Peoria High School
  • 1989 Graduate of Air Force Basic Training
  • 1999 Graduate of Illinois Central College
  • Current Bradley University Student

I graduated from Peoria High School in June of 1986. I enrolled at Eureka College in Eureka, Illinois in the fall of 1986. I played football at Eurka until an unfortuanate injury eneded my playing days.

In the fall of 1988 I enlisted in the U.S. Air Force and went to bascic training at Lackland Air Force Base in San Antonio, Texas. I spent time in the Air Force and Illinois Air National Guard. In the fall of 1990 I was activated for the first Gulf War. I served on active duty until July of 1991 when I was offered an early discharge from the service since they were trying to downsize after the war. I accepted their offer and was discharged.

After working different jobs for a few years I started back to school at Illinois Central College in the fall of 1997. I graduated from ICC with an Associate in Science with a concentration in Data Processing.

I began working at Bradley in the fall of 2000 and in the summer of 2002 I started to take calsses at Bradley. I have been working on my Bachelor degree ever since. I have taken classes as I have had time but am now only 18 hours away from getting my Bachelor degree.
